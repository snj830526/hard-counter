# Mechanical punch impact audio

The punch impact sounds in this directory use `thud2.wav` and `thud3.wav`
from Brian MacIntosh's **Metal Impact Sounds**, with only the first 70–80 ms
of Kenney's `impactMetal_heavy` samples retained for contact definition. The
long ringing tails are removed, and no explosion samples are used.
